I started Keystone Hardscapes because I was tired of seeing homeowners disappointed by contractors who overpromise and underdeliver. Too many companies hand off projects to subcontractors, prioritize speed over quality, or disappear after the final payment.

I take a different approach. As the owner and lead craftsman, I'm personally involved in every project from consultation to completion. I don't hire out your job to strangers—I'm on-site, ensuring every detail meets my standards.

My goal is simple: create outdoor spaces that last for decades, backed by honest communication and a 5-year warranty that I stand behind.

When you work with Keystone, you're working with me.

Built for Alberta's Climate

Alberta's freeze-thaw cycles, temperature swings, and harsh winters demand more than standard hardscaping techniques. I design and build every project with our climate in mind:

  • Proper drainage: Water management is critical to prevent frost heave and cracking.
  • Base preparation: Deep, properly compacted bases that can withstand freeze-thaw cycles.
  • Material selection: Products rated for Alberta's temperature extremes.
  • Seasonal planning: Timing projects to work with, not against, our climate.

This isn't just about building something that looks good today—it's about building something that performs for decades.
