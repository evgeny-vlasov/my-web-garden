How Route-Based Service Works

Simple, predictable snow removal all winter long

1. You're On the Route

Sign up for the season, and you're added to my route. No need to call every time it snows—you're covered automatically.

2. Snow Trigger

After snowfalls meeting the agreed trigger (typically 3-5cm), I run the route and clear all properties. Early morning service for driveways.

3. Cleared & Ready

You wake up (or arrive at work) to a cleared, accessible property. Simple billing, no surprises, consistent service.
