Concrete Restoration

Restore, don't replace. Professional concrete repair and refinishing.

Save Money, Extend Life

Cracked, stained, or worn concrete doesn't always need replacement. In many cases, professional restoration gives you a like-new surface for a fraction of the cost of tearing out and repouring.

At Keystone Hardscapes, I specialize in concrete restoration using epoxy overlays, crack repair, and surface treatments. Whether it's a garage floor, driveway, patio, or commercial space, I can often restore what you have instead of starting from scratch.

The result? Durable, attractive concrete that performs for years to come—without the cost and disruption of full replacement.

Why Restore Instead of Replace?

Cost Savings

Restoration typically costs 40-60% less than full replacement. You get a like-new surface without the expense of demo and new concrete.

Faster Timeline

Most restoration projects complete in 1-3 days. No waiting weeks for new concrete to cure—you're back to using the space quickly.

Less Disruption

No heavy equipment, no hauling away tons of concrete, minimal mess. Restoration is far less invasive than replacement.

Can Your Concrete Be Restored?

Not all concrete is a good candidate for restoration. I provide honest assessments of whether restoration makes sense or if replacement is the better option.

Good Candidates for Restoration:
  • Minor to moderate cracking
  • Surface staining or wear
  • Sound structural base
  • Cosmetic damage only
May Require Replacement:
  • Severe structural cracking
  • Major settling or heaving
  • Extensive rebar exposure
  • Complete surface deterioration
