Built for Alberta's Climate

Standard hardscaping techniques don't cut it in Calgary. Our freeze-thaw cycles, temperature extremes, and harsh winters demand a different approach:

  • Deep base preparation: 8-12 inches of compacted gravel base prevents frost heave
  • Proper drainage design: Water management is critical—pooling water destroys hardscaping in winter
  • Climate-rated materials: Pavers and stone selected for freeze-thaw resistance
  • Engineered slopes: Subtle grading directs water away from structures
  • Expansion joints: Allow for seasonal movement without cracking

This attention to climate-specific detail is why our hardscaping lasts 20+ years while shortcuts fail in 5.
