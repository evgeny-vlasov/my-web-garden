Comprehensive Therapeutic Support

Every individual's journey is unique. I offer personalized therapy services to help you navigate life's challenges and achieve your goals.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one therapy sessions provide a safe, confidential space to explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences. Whether you're dealing with specific challenges or seeking general personal growth, individual therapy offers personalized support tailored to your unique needs.

What to expect:

  • Confidential, judgment-free environment
  • Collaborative goal-setting
  • Evidence-based therapeutic techniques
  • Practical coping strategies
  • Support and guidance throughout your journey
Session Information
  • Duration: 50-minute sessions
  • Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly (as appropriate)
  • Rate: Contact for current rates
  • Insurance: Direct billing available for many providers

Areas of Focus

Anxiety & Stress Management

Support for generalized anxiety, panic attacks, social anxiety, and chronic stress. Learn effective coping strategies to manage worry and reduce anxiety's impact on your life.

Depression & Mood Support

Help with low mood, lack of motivation, and emotional difficulties. Work through depression with compassionate support and evidence-based therapeutic approaches.

Life Transitions

Navigate major life changes including career shifts, relationship changes, relocation, loss and grief, or other significant life events with guided support.

Relationship Issues

Work on communication skills, conflict resolution, boundary setting, and understanding relationship patterns to build healthier connections with others.

Work-Life Balance

Address burnout, professional stress, and work-related challenges. Develop strategies to achieve better balance and improve overall well-being.

Personal Growth

Explore self-esteem, identity, life purpose, and goal setting. Therapy for those seeking greater self-awareness and personal development.

Professional Consultation Services
