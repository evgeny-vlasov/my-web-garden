Frequently Asked Questions

Is therapy right for me?

Therapy can benefit anyone seeking support, personal growth, or help with specific challenges. A brief consultation can help determine if we're a good fit.

What about fees and insurance?

Session rates and insurance information will be discussed during your initial consultation. Many insurance plans cover psychotherapy services.

How do I schedule an appointment?

Contact us via phone, email, or the form above. We'll schedule a brief consultation to discuss your needs and find a convenient appointment time.

Is my information confidential?

Yes, all communications and therapy sessions are confidential and protected by professional ethics and privacy laws.
